I am
Tim Berners-Lee

I am the founder of www: World Wide Web

Hi!

Tim Berners-Lee invented the world Wide Web in 1989. Sir Tim is the Director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), a web standards organization foundedin 1994 which develops interoperable technologies (spcifications, guidelines, software and tools) to lead the Web to its full potentrial. He is a Director of the World Wide Web Foundation which was launched in 2009 to coordinate effects to further the potential of the Web of benefit humamity.

In 2001 he became a fellow of royal society. He has been the recipient several international awards including the Japan Prize, the prince of Asturius Foundation Prize, the Millennium Technology Prize, and Germary's Die Quadriga award. In 2004 he was knighted by H.M. Queen Elizabeth and in 2007 he was awarded the order of Merlit. In 2009 he was elected a foreign Sir Tim Berners-associate of the National Academy of sciences.
